# Problem
Users needed options for early withdrawal prior to maturity and term renewal (manual & auto-renewal) after maturity.

# Reason
Depositors may require liquidity before tenor completion, or may wish to roll over their principal into a new term without manually withdrawing and re-depositing.

# Solution
1. Added `earlyWithdraw()` function in `SavingCore.sol` applying early withdrawal penalty BPS, refunding `principal - penalty` to depositor and transferring penalty to `feeReceiver`.
2. Added `renewDeposit()` for manual term renewal within a 3-day grace period, disbursing interest for the completed term and minting a new NFT certificate.
3. Added `autoRenewDeposit()` and auto-renewal flag setters.

# Decision
Early withdrawal forfeits all interest and incurs a penalty. Manual renewal pays earned interest immediately and rolls over the principal into a new certificate position.
